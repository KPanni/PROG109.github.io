My Allergy Hypnosis Experiment

 

I have had a peanut allergy for as long as I can remember. When I was a baby, according to my mother, I she had tried to feed me peanut butter and I ended up getting sick. Ever since then I could not eat peanuts without my throat swelling up. So, one time in middle-school I thought of a way to cure myself. I had an idea that my peanut allergy might be psychosomatic due to my experience as a baby. So, I tried to cure this through by means of self-hypnosis. A post from the website https://www.skillsyouneed.com states the following about how self-hypnosis works:

Hypnotism works by reaching a relaxed state whereby it is possible to sink deeper into our minds and rewrite or reprogram our subconscious. Through physical and mental relaxation, self-hypnosis can allow people to bypass their conscious minds and introduce positive thoughts and ideas into their unconscious. Upon ‘awakening’ from the hypnotic state the new thoughts and ideas in the subconscious will, eventually, affect the conscious mind and can, in turn, lead to changed behaviors.

So through self-hypnosis I attempted to trick my brain into think I was eating sesame seeds, since to me peanuts and sesame seeds have a similar smell. I did, two or three hypnosis sessions on myself and then tried it out…and it worked!! For a month I was able to eat peanut butter, butterfingers, recess pieces, M&M’s …the peanut kind, and my favorite…. peanut brittle. For the first time I could finally understand what made peanut candies so wonderful, a food allergy truly is a curse. After that though the effects stopped, I tried a salted peanut from a bowl at my friend’s house, and my throat swelled up…. I was back to square. I haven’t tried the experiment again since, but who knows, if I keep up the trance maybe it the effects will work permanently one day.


My Ghost Hunting Experience

 

There was one time when one of my friends told me his house was haunted by ghost. He could hear odd bumps in his house, as well as spots in his house where it was unusually cold; even some of the electrical appliances would act funny. The thing is, this friend of mine used to ghost hunt in old places with some friends of his and could recognize when certain phenomena that indicated the presence of a spirit; even his girlfriend at the time was starting to get headaches whenever she would stay in the room where most of the haunting was happening. What ever this spirit was doing it was trying to get their attention. My friend was relaying all this to over the phone and had recognized this ghost to be some sort of trickster. This made it even more convincing as the sound would cut in and out of our conversation as well as we would hear an unusual static like voice in the background; first on my end of the phone line then on my friend’s. Since it was becoming more active we tried to investigate the following week. We started at around 11pm a week later, I brought my laptop which had a recording program on it. We tried recording some of the sounds while were in the room, trying to call out to it and ask it question (my friend was trying to intimidate the spirit, not a smart thing to do in my opinion).

While we were doing this, we made sure all the lights were off in the house apart from the light from our phone and devices. After a couple of hours things started to get weird: we started to hear bumps and creeks every 10 minutes, my friend started getting a headache all of a sudden (moral: don’t be rude to ghosts), and in the kitchen my friends freezer started humming and turned on while it was working….my friend broken freezer. After we were done investigating, we played back our recording and amplified some of the subtler noises, turns out the ghost answered some of our questions, from what we could make out from the sound the ghost was female when she was alive named Hilary and that she was stuck there. After that we made an attempted to cleans the house with sage and had poured some holy water on my friend’s head, we also tried to open a window and light a candle to get the ghost out (this worked in a previous ghost encounter I had, but that’s another story). Not sure whether it worked or not, my friend moved out of that house shortly after, but it was one adventure I won’t forget.
